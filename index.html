Lets go on Vacation!

I love going on Vacation,!

Lets go to RIP CITY and check out some of the wonderful places Portland, OR has to offer:

Parks & Recreation

  • Take a stroll and smell the flowers at the Portland Rose Garden (NW pdx)
  • Walk around Mt. Tabor (SE pdx) and take in the wonderful view of SE Portland.
  • Sunbathe in the beautiful Laurelhurst Park. (SE pdx)

Restaurants and Nightlife

  • Quinch your thirst with a delicious cocktail and enjoy some wonder DJ night at the Lowtide Lounge(SE pdx)
  • Check out the VEGAN METAL PUNK bar & venue for all your comfort food and live music needs at Blackwater Bar & Grill(NE pdx)
  • Enjoy some tasty Tiki cocktails and Hawaiian grub at Da Hui' Bar & Grill (SE pdx)
